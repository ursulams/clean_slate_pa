Check if your Pennsylvania record can be sealed

Under Pennsylvania's Clean Slate Act, many convictions can be sealed or erased — which means they won't show up on most background checks for jobs and housing.

This check is free, private, and instant. We look up public court records using your name and date of birth. We don't store your information.

💼 Know your employment rights

  • Statewide: Pennsylvania's Ban the Box law means public employers cannot ask about your record until later in the hiring process.
  • Philadelphia: Employers cannot ask about your record until after a conditional job offer. As of January 2026, they can only look back 4 years for misdemeanors and 7 years for felonies.
  • Learn your rights at pubintlaw.org →
